About The Founder

Udo Emmanuel is a foreign exchange trader and portfolio manager at TradingExperience with four years’ experience helping clients with investment decision-making. Specializing in CFDs, Udo uses that experience to devise and implement investment strategies and processes to meet client goals and constraints, construct and manage portfolios, make decisions on what and when to buy and sell investments.

By focusing on clients’ satisfaction, Udo has been able to put the experience he gained to good use. Over the years, His strengths in portfolio management and foreign exchange trading has garnered some recognition for wealth creation and poverty alleviation. Udo may spend his days at TradingExperience, but it’s his vision of wealth creation and clients’ satisfaction that gets him up in the morning.

When he is not at TradingExperience, Udo is an avid reader and loves spending time keeping abreast of all tech innovations and revolution. He manages and directs the company toward its primary goals and objectives. Oversees employment decisions at the executive level of the company. Leads a team of executives to consider major decisions including acquisitions, mergers, joint ventures, or large-scale expansion.
